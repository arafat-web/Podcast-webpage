Dr. Shari D. Scott

Dr. Shari D. Scott is an ordained minister, a published author, and the founder of Developing Healthy Desires and Behaviors Ministry (DHDB); a ministry devoted to seeing the broken-hearted healed. With the help of the Holy Ghost, Dr. Scott teaches people how to apply biblical truth to every aspect of their lives.

Fueled by her love for Christ and passion for souls, Dr. Scott desire to expose the lies, tricks and deception of the enemy; in particular to men and women who are currently suffering from the aftermath of spiritual, emotional, verbal and sexual abuse. She has two earned Doctorate degrees (one in Biblical Counseling and the other in Psychology). Dr. Scott operates under the premise that the solutions to all of the problems we face in life are rooted in discovering the love of Christ, receiving his forgiveness and obeying His Word.

In her books, Dr. Scott candidly shares her life experiences in hopes that others who have encountered sexual abuse, molestation, rejection, mistreatment, shame, verbal abuse, loneliness and pain will gain hope, insight and ultimately freedom from their malevolent affects. Dr. Scott ultimate aim is to promote the love of God, to display the character of Jesus Christ, and to enhance personal relationships with God through Bible Studies, prayer meetings, and Christian fellowships.
